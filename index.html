How do we structure the day?

1. Training

We teach all the necessary things to jump from the planbe without any kind of problem.

2. Get ready

You are already prepared. You just need a suit and a parachute. All sizes available.

3. Fly

You are ready and the plane is waiting for us in the hangar. Let's fly!

4. Jump!

You have done the hardest part. Just one step left... jump!
